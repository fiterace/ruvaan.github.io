I am interested. How to Register? 😕


It's Simple. Click on the register button on any event. Fill all the details in Google Form. Pay registration fees. Done.
Your registration will be confirmed only after you pay the required fee. Only team leader should register for the team events.

👍










But, how am I going to stay? 🤔


Don't worry. You will provided blanket, mattress, and food coupons for your two days stay. You need to book your accomodation in advance from above link. You will be asked to pay a nominal INR 500 fees for the accomodation.

👍










How do you reach IIT Mandi? Will I have to hike my way there? 😫


Luckily, you will not have to hike. You can reach Mandi easily from Delhi, Chandigarh, etc. by bus. From there you can take institute bus to reach the college. college bus schedule

👍










How is the weather? Will i have to dress like an Eskimo? 🥶


Luckily you will not have to dress like an Eskimo!
But the temperature is quite low, around 10°C to 15°C. So you are advised to bring warm clothes accordingly. Also bring an umbrella, coz you never know when it's gonna rain.
